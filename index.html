I'm a U.S. Marine Corps veteran and cleared systems administrator building a focused career transition into identity and access management (ICAM) and federal security engineering. I hold an active TS/SCI CI Polygraph clearance and have spent the past several years working inside classified federal environments at the intersection of identity, infrastructure, and security.

My background includes enterprise Active Directory administration, RBAC enforcement, PKI certificate management, privileged access provisioning, endpoint hardening aligned to DISA STIGs, and vulnerability remediation across mission-critical federal networks. I've supported FBI infrastructure, performed patch captain duties, and worked alongside ISSOs on compliance-driven security operations.

I built this platform to document my technical journey in public: the home lab work, the scripting, the ICAM tooling, and the road to security engineering. I also want it to be a resource for other veterans and cleared professionals who are navigating this same transition without a clear roadmap.

If you're a vet breaking into federal IT, a cleared professional targeting ICAM or security engineering roles, or a hiring manager looking to connect, reach out.
